🔒 Your Base Model is Always Safe

Fine-tuning creates a completely separate model file in a new folder. Your original best_model/ is never touched or overwritten.

Every organization gets its own isolated model. Fine-tuning one organization's data never affects another's model performance.

⚡ Freeze BERT vs Full Fine-Tune

Freeze BERT (recommended for <500 samples) — Only trains the classification heads. Prevents overfitting on small datasets. Faster training.

Full Fine-Tune (for 500+ samples) — Trains all layers including the BERT backbone. Higher accuracy ceiling but requires more labeled data.
